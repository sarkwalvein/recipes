- 5 potatoes
- 2 onions
- a lot of olive oil
- 5 eggs
- salt and pepper

Directions:
1. Cut the potatos in slices
2. Cut the onion in Juliana style
3. Heat a lot of olive oil in a frying pan at medium heat
4. Fry the onions and potatos
5. Crack the eggs and put them all on a big bowl
6. Take the potatos an onions out of the frying pan and mix them with the eggs
7. Put the mix again into the frying pan, with little to no oil
8. Wait for it to brown, turn it around and repeat
9. Profit
